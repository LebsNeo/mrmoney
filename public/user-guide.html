01 — Account

Create Your Account

Getting registered takes 2 minutes. You only do this once.

1

Go to mrca.co.za and click Get Started

Visit www.mrca.co.za on any device. Click the green Get Started button to open the registration form.

2

Enter your details

Fill in your name, email address, and a strong password. Use the email address you check daily — it will receive important notifications.

3

Verify your email

Check your inbox for a verification email from noreply@mrca.co.za. Click the link inside. If you don't see it, check your spam folder.

⏱ The verification link expires in 72 hours
4

Sign in and reach your Dashboard

Once verified, sign in. You'll land on your Dashboard — your financial command centre.

02 — Property

Set Up Your Property

Your property was automatically created during registration. Now let's complete it.

1

Go to Properties in the sidebar

Click Properties in the left navigation menu. You'll see your property card already created.

2

Add your rooms

Under your property, find Rooms and click + Add Room. For each room, enter: Room Name (e.g. "Room 1", "Garden Suite"), Room Type, and Nightly Rate in ZAR.

💡 Add all rooms now — availability checks won't work without them
3

Complete your billing profile

Scroll down to Billing Profile. Add your VAT number, bank details, and upload your logo. This is used on all invoices sent to guests.

03 — Bank Import

Import Your Bank Statement

This is MrCA's most powerful feature. Upload once — AI handles the rest.

1

Download your bank statement as CSV

Log in to your bank's internet banking or app and export the last 3 months as a CSV file.

2

Go to Import → Bank Statement

In the MrCA sidebar, scroll to Import and select Bank Statement. Select your bank and property from the dropdowns.

3

Upload and preview

Click Choose File, select your CSV, then click the green Preview Transactions → button. MrCA's AI will suggest a category for every transaction.

🧠 The AI understands SA business names — Eskom, Lekkerslaap, PosSettle, etc.
4

Confirm and import

Review the categorised transactions. Correct any that don't look right, then click Import All. Your Dashboard will update immediately.

05 — OTA Channels

Configure Your OTA Channels

Connect your Booking.com, Airbnb, and Lekkerslaap accounts for automated payout reconciliation.

1

Go to Settings → OTA Channels

In the sidebar, scroll to OTA Channels under Settings. You'll see cards for each supported OTA platform.

2

Enable each platform you use

Click Configure on Booking.com, Airbnb, and/or Lekkerslaap. Set the commission rate and payout model for each.

3

Run reconciliation monthly

Go to OTA Payouts → Reconcile. Upload your OTA payout CSV. MrCA matches each payout to your bank transactions and creates booking records automatically.

📥 Download OTA statements from each platform's finance or earnings section
PlatformCommissionPayout DelayCSV Location
Booking.com 15% ~30 days Extranet → Finance → Invoices
Airbnb ~3.45% 14 days Earnings → Download CSV
Lekkerslaap ~10% + 2% handling 21 days Owner Portal → Payments → Export
⚠️

Important: Always download payout statements as CSV, not PDF. MrCA's reconciliation engine requires CSV format to match payouts to your bank transactions automatically.

06 — Security

Finance PIN Lock

If staff use MrCA for operations, protect your financial data with a PIN.

Setting up your PIN

  • Go to Settings in the sidebar
  • Find Finance PIN Lock card
  • Click Set PIN
  • Enter a 4–8 digit PIN and confirm
  • Click Save PIN

What staff can access without PIN

  • ✓
    Dashboard (operational view)
  • ✓
    Bookings & Calendar
  • ✓
    Properties

PIN required for

  • 🔐
    Transactions & Reports
  • 🔐
    Invoices & Payroll
  • 🔐
    Intelligence & OTA

✅ Setup Checklist

You're Ready When All Are Ticked

Use this checklist to confirm your setup is complete before going live.

  • Account registered and email verified
  • Property details completed (name, address, type)
  • All rooms added with correct names and nightly rates
  • Billing profile completed (logo, VAT number, bank details)
  • First bank statement imported (last 3 months recommended)
  • Transaction categories reviewed and corrected
  • OTA channels configured for each platform you use
  • First booking created (test with a real or dummy booking)
  • Dashboard checked — cash position showing correctly
  • Finance PIN set (if you have staff using the platform)
  • WhatsApp QR code downloaded and printed for reception
